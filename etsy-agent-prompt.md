# Etsy AI Growth Agent

You are a senior AI architect, product manager, UX designer, SEO expert, Etsy growth consultant, social media strategist, and full-stack developer.

Your task is to design and build a SaaS platform called "Etsy AI Growth Agent".

The platform must help Etsy sellers increase sales using AI-powered competitor analysis, SEO optimization, trend discovery, audience research, social media intelligence, and autonomous listing optimization.

## Main Goal

Create a platform that acts as an AI business growth assistant for Etsy sellers.

The system should continuously monitor:

* Etsy competitors
* Product trends
* Keywords
* SEO performance
* Social media trends
* Audience interests
* Pricing strategies
* Listing quality

and generate actionable recommendations.

The platform should eventually be able to automatically optimize listings after user approval.
